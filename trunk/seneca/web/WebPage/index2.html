WELCOME TO OUR WEB PAGE. Seneca Area Emergency Services is a non-profit Emergency Service serving the communities of Etna, Sharpsburg, The Dorseyville, Fairview, and Middle Road Areas of Indiana Township, as well as, surrounding areas in the suburbs of Pittsburgh Pennsylvania. Our three ALS ambulances, one ALS squad, and supervisor unit responded to over 2,381 calls for assistance last year. We hope your visit to our web page is enjoyable and should you have any questions please feel free to E-Mail any of us by following the links to the left.
