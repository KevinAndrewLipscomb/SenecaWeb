Seneca Area Operations Report

 

What follows is a summary of activity for Seneca Area Emergency Services. 

2001 Accomplishments Include:

 

                                        -     Responded to 2312 calls for assistance

                                        -     Installed over 700 Smoke Alarms free of charge

                                        -    Provided 28 Free CPR Classes

                                        -    Provided 16 First Aid Classes

                                        -    Provided Local Police Departments with AED Training

                                        -    Moved into and Remodeled our new quarters

                                        -    Instituted an aggressive patient survey project

                                        -    Joined with the Allegheny County Department of Emergency Services

                                              To house the Silver team local haz-mat team
